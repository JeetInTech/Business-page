3. Delivery Timeline

Standard Delivery: Instant to 5 minutes after successful payment confirmation

What happens after purchase:

  • Payment Processing: 1-2 minutes (via Razorpay)
  • System Verification: 1-2 minutes
  • Product Delivery: Immediate to 5 minutes
  • Email Confirmation: Within 5 minutes

Delayed Delivery: If you don't receive your product within 15 minutes, please check your spam folder or contact our support team.

Since we operate exclusively in the digital space:

  • No courier or postal services are involved
  • No packaging or handling fees

To ensure successful delivery and product usage:

  • Valid Email: Provide an active email address for delivery
  • Internet Connection: Required for download and access
  • Compatible Device: Ensure your device meets product requirements
  • Updated Browser: For web-based products and account access

6. Access and Download
