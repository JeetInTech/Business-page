Cancellation and Refund Policy

Last Updated: June 6, 2025

1. Digital Products Policy

Important Notice: Due to the digital nature of our products (software, code, and digital subscriptions), all sales are considered final once the product has been delivered and accessed.

Digital products cannot be "returned" in the traditional sense as they are delivered instantly and remain accessible after purchase. Once you download or access our digital products, the transaction is complete and non-refundable.

2. No Physical Shipping

Jeet Enterprises operates as a purely digital business. We do not ship physical products or handle physical deliveries. All our products and services are delivered electronically through:

  • Email delivery of download links
  • Account dashboard access
  • Instant software activation
  • Direct download from our platform

3. Subscription Cancellations

Good News: You can cancel your subscription at any time with no cancellation fees.

How to Cancel:

  • Log into your account dashboard
  • Navigate to "Subscription Management"
  • Click "Cancel Subscription"
  • Confirm your cancellation

Cancellation Terms:

  • Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period
  • You retain access to all features until the subscription expires
  • No refunds for partial subscription periods
  • You can reactivate your subscription at any time

4. Exceptional Refund Circumstances

While our general policy is no refunds for digital products, we may consider refunds in the following exceptional cases:

  • Technical Issues: If our product has significant technical defects that prevent its intended use
  • Billing Errors: If you were charged incorrectly due to our system error
  • Duplicate Charges: If you were accidentally charged multiple times for the same product
  • Unauthorized Transactions: If charges were made without your authorization

Refund Timeframe: All refund requests must be submitted within 7 days of purchase and include detailed explanation of the issue.

5. Refund Process

If you believe you qualify for a refund under our exceptional circumstances:

  • Contact our support team at support@jeetenterprises.com
  • Provide your order number and payment details
  • Explain the specific issue in detail
  • Provide screenshots or evidence if applicable

Processing Time: Approved refunds will be processed within 5-10 business days through the original payment method (Razorpay).

6. What We Cannot Refund

No Refunds For:

  • Change of mind after product access
  • Compatibility issues with your system (check requirements before purchase)
  • Inability to use the product due to lack of technical knowledge
  • Partial subscription periods
  • Products purchased more than 7 days ago
  • Custom development work or personalized solutions

7. Payment Gateway Policy

All payments are processed through Razorpay, which is PCI DSS compliant and secure. Razorpay's refund policies also apply to transactions made through our platform.

Razorpay Refund Timeline:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5-7 business days
  • Net Banking: 5-7 business days
  • UPI: 1-3 business days
  • Wallets: 1-3 business days

8. Chargeback Policy

Initiating a chargeback or dispute without first contacting us may result in:

  • Immediate suspension of your account
  • Revocation of access to purchased products
  • Legal action for breach of terms

Please contact us first - we're committed to resolving issues fairly and quickly.

9. Contact for Support

Before considering any refund request, please reach out to our support team. We're here to help resolve any issues you may have with our products or services.
